### Hi there 👋

I'm Manuel Rabade, Computer Engineer.

🛠️ I'm currently working on

- [Homelab](/manuel-rabade/homelab) is a guide of my personal infraestructure.

💾 Projects of the past that still shine

- [Sismo Alerta](/manuel-rabade/sismo_alerta) is an open-source receiver for the Mexican earthquake alert system.
- [Arduinos on the Raspberry Pi](/manuel-rabade/arduino-chibios-rpi) addresses the Space Apps 2013 Ardusat challenge to emulate several Arduino boards using a Linux computer.

🤖 I love to do hardware and software projects

- [Pomodoro Arduino](/manuel-rabade/pomodoro_arduino) is a Pomodoro timer built around an Arduino Pro Mini.
- [ADC Pi MPX5050](/manuel-rabade/adc_pi_mpx5050) and [PCB MPX20xx](/manuel-rabade/pcb_mpx20xx) are experiments to measure water depth by pressure.
- [Vídeo compuesto y Arduino](/manuel-rabade/composite_video) are examples to generate composite video and audio on an Arduino.

👾 I made innovative web apps during my fellowship at the [Laboratorio para la Ciudad](/LabPLC)

- [Verifícalo](/manuel-rabade/verificalo) pulls information from different government agencies to help Mexico city drivers comply with their obligations.
- [Mi Ecobici](/manuel-rabade/mi_ecobici) let users of the Mexico city bike sharing platform to visualize their trips and environmental impact.
- [La resaca del DF](/manuel-rabade/la_resaca_del_df) explores the relationship between environmental contingencies and festivities in Mexico city.
