### Hi there 👋

I'm Manuel Rabade, Computer Engineer.

🛠️ I'm currently working on

- [Homelab](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/homelab) is a configuration guide for my personal infraestructure.
- [Dotfiles](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/home-dotfiles) because home is where my dotfiles are.

💾 Projects of the past that still shine

- [Sismo Alerta](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/sismo_alerta) is an open-source receiver for the Mexican earthquake alert system.
- [Arduinos on the Raspberry Pi](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/arduino-chibios-rpi) addresses the Space Apps 2013 Ardusat challenge to emulate several Arduino boards using a Linux computer.

👾 I built some innovative web apps during my fellowship at the [Laboratorio para la Ciudad](https://github.com/LabPLC)

- [Verifícalo](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/verificalo) pulls information from different government agencies to help Mexico City drivers comply with their obligations.
- [Mi Ecobici](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/mi_ecobici) let users of the Mexico City bike sharing platform to visualize their trips and environmental impact.
- [La resaca del DF](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/la_resaca_del_df) explores the relationship between environmental contingencies and festivities in Mexico City.

🤖 Exploring the realm of hardware and software

- [Pomodoro Arduino](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/pomodoro_arduino) is a Pomodoro timer built around an Arduino Pro Mini.
- [ADC Pi MPX5050](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/adc_pi_mpx5050) and [PCB MPX20xx](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/pcb_mpx20xx) are experiments to measure water depth by pressure.
- [Vídeo compuesto y Arduino](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/composite_video) are examples to generate composite video and audio on an Arduino.
- [Raspberry Pi NATbox](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/rpi_natbox) and [Raspberry Pi Torrentbox](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/rpi_torrentbox) are configuration guides for Raspberry Pi.

🎓 Cool academic legacies

- [Von Neumann Microprocessor in VHDL](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/von_neumann), my final project for the Computer Architecture course.
- [Merkle-Hellman encryption system](https://github.com/manuel-rabade/merkle-hellman) didactic implementation. My final project for the Discrete Structures course.
